Incheon International Airport is the main part of arrival for most flights from outside Korea.
Located approximately 1 hour from downtown Seoul,
there are a number of convenient ways to get to the city from the airport.

Airport Bus

  • Airport buses travel to all major areas of Seoul. Tickets are KRW 12,000 to 18,000 and the travel time is approximately 60 to 80 minutes, depending on traffic and the destination.
  • Tickets can be purchased at designated ticket outside the passenger terminal, or fare can be paid in cash, T-money, or credit card (at Incheon Airport).
