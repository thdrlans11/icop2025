Presentation Guidelines

M2community International Conference 2024 provides authors with a chance to present their abstracts to a wider audience through oral and/or poster presentations.
To ensure that instructions are shared effectively with all the presenting authors of the accepted abstracts, we encourage all presenters to review the following:

  • Guidelines for Oral Presentation
  • Guideline for Poster Exhibition

Oral Presentation [Free Paper, NOT Invited Speaker]

Check Oral Presentation List
  • Important Notes
    • All oral presenters will be requested to attend the conference and deliver a presentation from the podium. The real-time question and answer session will be carried following each presentation.
  • How to Prepare the Oral Presentation
    • Each presenter will be allocated 10 minutes in total (7 minutes for the presentation, followed by 3 minutes for Q&A).
    • Please ensure that you strictly adhere to the allotted time.
    • All presentations must be conducted in English.
    • It is NOT allowed to use a personal laptop for presentation.
  • Presentation File Format
    Presentation File Format
    File format Microsoft PowerPoint
    Screen ratio 16:9
    Font Use basic fonts such as Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman, etc.
    Presentation Template Presentation Template Download
  • Equipment
    • A monitor, mouse and Keyboard will be provided on the podium for speakers.
      * No smart pointer on the podium
    • For the convenience of speakers, we will provide a slide note function so that you can see the script in the PPT which you prepared.
    • The use of personal laptops and Macs in the session room is not recommended.
      * If you wish to present with your own laptop (IBM or Mac), please visit the Preview Room at least 2 hours prior to your scheduled session.
      * If you use your own Macintosh with our beam projector, we ask to bring your Mac RGB Connector as we don’t provide any devices for Macintosh.
  • Submit Your Presentation File(s)
    • Please visit the Preview Room at least 1 hour before your scheduled presentation and bring your presentation file(s) on a USB memory stick.
    • Presentation slides should be prepared as an MS PowerPoint file 2003, 2007 or 2010, 2013 versions. If you have any other types of presentation files, please inform our Preview
    • Room staff in advance.
    • If your file contains audio or video, please inform our staff and check it with the system in advance.

Poster Exhibition

Check Poster Presentation List
  • How to Prepare the Poster Exhibition
    * Please prepare a poster and the size of poster should be smaller than 90 cm wide x 150 cm high.
    • Posters must be readable by viewers 1-2 meters away.
    • The message should be clear and understandable without oral explanation.
    • The poster title, author(s)’s name(s), and affiliation(s) should appear at the top.
    • Contents should be in the order of abstract.
    • Each poster should be placed on the numbered board assigned to each presenter.
    • Posters should be brought to the meeting and not mailed, as the organizing committee cannot be responsible for loss or mishandling.
    • Presenters are responsible for posting and removing their own materials.
    • Handouts are encouraged for participants wishing to receive more information about your poster and research.
    • Note that landscape posters or larger posters will not fit on the poster display boards and therefore, cannot be mounted.
